Frequently Asked Questions

Can I play Minecraft on my phone?

Minecraft is designed to work on both desktop and mobile browsers. For the smoothest experience on a phone or tablet, try rotating your screen or using the fullscreen button if the game supports it.

Is Minecraft suitable for school or work computers?

Minecraft is hosted as a standard web page with no installation requirements, which makes it compatible with most school and office browsers that allow general web access.

Why won't Minecraft load?

If the game window stays blank, try refreshing the page, disabling any ad blockers temporarily, or switching to an up-to-date browser like Chrome, Edge, or Firefox. A slow connection can also delay the initial load.
